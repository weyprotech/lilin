Lilin History Description

 
 

  • 1980

    • LILIN was founded in New Taipei City, Taiwan

  • 1981

    • LILIN launched its first scanner – PIH-035

  • 1986

    • LILIN launched its first video switcher – PIH-200

  • 1988

    • LILIN launched its first B/W monitor – PIH-09

  • 1989

    • LILIN launched its first B/W CCD camera – PIH-703

  • 1994

    • LILIN launched its first matrix – PIH-816

  • 1997

    • ISO 9001, year of 1994 certified
    • LILIN launched its first speed dome – PIH-717

  • 2001

    • LILIN launched its first DVR – PDR1600

  • 2003

    • LILIN launched its first IP Fast Dome – PIH7625IP
    • New factory building of LILIN H.Q. established in Wu-Ku Industrial Park of Taipei County, Taiwan
    • ISO 9001, year of 2000 certified

  • 2005

    • LILIN launched CMX Software 1.0

  • 2009

    • ISO 9001, ISO 2008 certified
    • LILIN launched the world's first range of ONVIF-based IP Cameras

  • 2010

    • LILIN launched true pure Hybrid DVR device

  • 2011

    • LILIN launched the world’s first 60M Full HD IP camera
    • LILIN delivered its first Full HD IP cameras – IPG1022

  • 2012

    • LILIN launched the world’s first embedded 1080P NVR Touch series

  • 2013

    • Auto Focus network cameras released – Z series
    • Fisheye Panorama 360° IP camera released – FD2452

  • 2014

    • Released 120FPS at 1080P IP camera – UFG 1122
    • 4K Ultra High Definition network cameras released – UHG1182
