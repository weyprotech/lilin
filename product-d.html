4K Day & Night IP Box Camera

Day & Night 1080P HD IP Camera

Model：LB1022X

• Full HD 2 megapixel CMOS image sensor
• True H.264 AVC/MPEG-4 part 10
• H.264 and Motion JPEG triple video streaming
• 3D noise reduction (MCTF)
• 2D WDR function

• Digital zoom supported
• Audio / Motion detection alarm function
• Day & Night (IR cut removable)
• 2-way audio supported
• Digital I/O : 1 in, 1 out
• ONVIF supported

LB1022X in the L series is featuring built-in Sense Up+ technology to deliver stunning video in low-light conditions. Utilizing intelligent image signal processing, 2D WDR function, AGC control, and 3D noise reduction, the combination successfully delivers the ultimate low-light image without motion blur.
With built-in IR-cut filter, white balance, back light compensation, and IVS features such as audio and motion detection. Featuring a high-end feature such as Micro SD/SDHC card slot for data redundancy. The LB1022X camera is the ideal choice for the most demanding monitoring applications, and it can secure a variety of sites.
